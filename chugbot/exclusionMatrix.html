Welcome to the Exclusion Matrix

This page lets you tell the system that certain chugim should not be assigned to the same camper in the same block as other chugim. For example, you might want to prevent a camper from being assigned to both Cooking and Outdoor Cooking.

To disallow a chug combination, find the first chug on the left side, and match it to the column headed by the second chug. For example, to prevent the system from assigning Cooking and Outdoor Cooking to the same camper in the same block, you would find the row with "Cooking" at the start, then find the column with "Outdoor Cooking" at the top, and click the checkbox at the intersection.

When you are done, click "Save Changes" at the bottom of this page.
